Soccer

It's probably the most important sport of the earth, sport that blend emotions, logic, money, and a lot of things more.

How to play ?

The game is made up of two teams with eleven players each, two goal lines, a ball, and sometimes three referees

The objective of the game is to score goal on the goal lines, the game is divided in two parts, each part with duration of forty-five minutes
